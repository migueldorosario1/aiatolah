Indonesia is taking a strong stance on the importance of inclusive governance and digital sovereignty as it relates to artificial intelligence development within the ASEAN region. By prioritizing these principles, Indonesia aims to ensure that technological advancements benefit all segments of society, not just a privileged few.

The push for inclusive governance reflects a broader understanding that AI should serve the needs of diverse populations. This approach is particularly important in a region like ASEAN, where varying economic and social conditions exist across member states.

Digital sovereignty is another key aspect of Indonesia's vision, emphasizing that countries should have control over their own data and technology. This is vital for protecting national interests and fostering an environment where local innovations can thrive, rather than being overshadowed by foreign tech giants.

As Indonesia leads these discussions, it sets a precedent for other ASEAN nations to follow. By advocating for policies that promote equitable access to AI technologies, the country is positioning itself as a leader in the region's digital transformation.

Moreover, inclusive governance can enhance trust among citizens, which is essential for the successful implementation of AI initiatives. When people feel their voices are heard and represented, they are more likely to embrace technological changes that affect their lives.

In conclusion, Indonesia's emphasis on inclusive governance and digital sovereignty in AI development is a crucial step for the ASEAN region. This approach not only aims to bridge the digital divide but also empowers countries to take charge of their technological futures.
